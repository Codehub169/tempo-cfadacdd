The hueneu Story

Hueneu is born from a dialogue between Hue—the vibrant bursts of creative color, the unexpected palettes that tell a story—and Neu—the grounding force of neutrality, the calm, intentional space that allows stories to breathe and resonate.

We believe in design that is quiet but bold, calm yet mysterious, and always a little playful. It's about creating experiences that surprise with those unexpected design moments, the ones that make you think... “Who Knew?”
